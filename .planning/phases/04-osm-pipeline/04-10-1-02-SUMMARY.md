# Phase 4 Plan 04-10.1 Wave 2: Feldweg-drop Summary

**One-liner:** Feldweg (`highway=track|path`) dropped from `osm.sqlite` ways table; still emitted to pmtiles `roads` layer for REN-02 base geometry. Berlin `osm.sqlite` shrank from 84.8 MB → 49.1 MB (−39.4% / clears the `<50 MB` gate). `pipelineSchemaVersion` bumped to 2. Six planning-doc narrowing edits landed (OSM-02, REN-02, MMT-05 NOTE, P4-SC2, P7-SC1, STATE Pending Todo).

Research §3.3 projected `~50%` shrink; actual `39.4%` is inside the 20% margin the plan's Deviations section allows (escalation trigger was `> 60 MB`, at 49.06 MiB we're well under). No escalation.
